About Us

Our Story

For over 12 years, GeoScape Co. has been at the forefront of providing an extensive range of urban furniture, play, sports, and fitness equipment, as well as wood plastic composites. We are dedicated to transforming outdoor spaces with products that embody creativity, innovation, and the highest standards of quality. Our partnerships with distinguished manufacturers from around the globe ensure that we deliver durability and sustainability in with Saudi Arabia’s VISION 2030.

Our Services

At GeoScape, we offer a comprehensive suite of professional landscape services designed to meet the highest standards of quality. We leverage the best products available both in the Saudi Arabian and international markets to ensure excellence in every project. Our services include: Landscape Design: We create detailed 2D and 3D designs to bring your vision to life. Installation: Our team handles the complete installation process, guaranteeing the quality and longevity of our products and services. Maintenance Plans: We offer a variety of maintenance plans tailored to fit your specific needs, ensuring your landscape remains pristine year-round.

Our Mission

To be an integral part of our clients’ daily lives by offering top-tier local services, delivering professional technological solutions, and fostering relationships based on trust and reliability.

Our Commitment

Quality: We are committed to providing the highest quality products and services. Our rigorous standards ensure that every project we undertake is executed to perfection.
